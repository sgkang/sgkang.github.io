About

Introduction to geophysics for geology majors. Covers gravimetry, geomagnetism, and seismics.

Part of the old curriculum (last taught in 2014). The new one has two courses for geophysics:

  • Geofísica 1: covers gravity and magnetics.
  • Geofísica 2: covers seismology, seismics, electric and electromagnetic methods.
